Privacy Policy

How Regma IT AB handles your personal data under GDPR.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data controller

Regma IT AB, registered in Angered, Gothenburg, Sweden (SNI 62020), is the data controller for personal data processed through this website.

2. What data we collect

We collect data you provide directly:

  • Contact form: name, email, message
  • Account registration: name, email, password (hashed)
  • Job applications: name, email, CV/resume, experience details, skills
  • Profile information: phone number, LinkedIn URL (optional)

3. Why we process your data

  • To respond to your contact inquiries (legitimate interest)
  • To process job applications (consent / pre-contractual measures)
  • To manage your account (contract performance)
  • To improve our website and services (legitimate interest)

4. Data storage and retention

Your data is stored securely. Contact submissions are kept for 12 months. Job applications are kept for 24 months after the position is filled. Account data is kept until you request deletion.

5. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and preference storage (e.g. language selection, cookie consent choice). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Third-party services

We use Google Fonts for typography. No other third-party tracking services are active on this site.

8. Contact

For data requests or questions about this policy, contact us via the contact page.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
